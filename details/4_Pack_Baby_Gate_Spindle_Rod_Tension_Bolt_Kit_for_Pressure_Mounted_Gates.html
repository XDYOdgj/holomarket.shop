Baby Gate Replacement Parts Kit – Secure and Durable

Keep your home safe with this 4-pack replacement hardware kit tailored for pressure-mounted baby and pet gates. Featuring threaded spindle rods and tension bolts, it ensures a snug, reliable fit without compromising on quality or ease of use.

Built for Strength and Longevity

Each component of this baby gate replacement parts kit is designed to offer maximum durability while maintaining flexibility. The threaded spindle rods with an 8mm (M8) diameter provide a secure connection, and the adjustable length of 9cm (3.54\") allows you to customize the gate size to match your home's layout.

Safe Installation, Every Time

The anti-slip gel knot pads are crafted to shield walls from scratches and offer a firm grip, making installation and daily use safer for you and your family. Pair this with high-quality screw bolts for a long-lasting, stable setup that won’t weaken over time.

Who Can Benefit?

This baby gate replacement parts kit is perfect for parents, dog owners, and pet lovers who need a reliable solution for maintaining or upgrading their safety gates. Whether your gate is broken, missing parts, or needs an extension, this essential kit ensures everything fits like new and lasts for years.

Key Features

  • 4-piece replacement hardware kit – Ideal for pressure-mounted gates and wall installations.
  • Threaded spindle rods with 8mm (M8) diameter – Offer a secure fit without damaging your walls.
  • Anti-slip gel knot pads – Provide safety and protect your surfaces during setup and use.
  • Adjustable 9cm (3.54\") length – Let you tweak the gate width to suit your home.
  • High-quality screw bolts – Built to last, ensuring long-term durability and stability.

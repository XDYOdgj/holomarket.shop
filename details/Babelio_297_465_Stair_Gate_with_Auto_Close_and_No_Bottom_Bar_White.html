Keep Your Home Safe with the Babelio Stair Gate

Designed with safety in mind, the Babelio Stair Gate is the ideal solution for protecting your home from curious toddlers and playful pets. Whether you're looking to secure a staircase, hallway, or doorway, this gate offers versatile installation and durable performance to suit your needs.

With an adjustable width range of 29.7 to 46.5 inches, it adapts easily to different doorways and spaces, making it a practical choice for any home. The no bottom bar design is a thoughtful addition that reduces the risk of tripping, particularly at the top of stairs, and ensures a smoother path for everyone in your household.

The Babelio Stair Gate features an auto-close mechanism that closes effortlessly behind you, providing a seamless safety experience.

Babelio Adjustable Baby Gate

Keep your home safe and secure with the Babelio baby gate, designed to offer reliable protection for toddlers and pets. With an adjustable width that fits openings from 26 to 43 inches, this gate is perfect for stairs, doorways, and hallway areas. The 28-inch height provides the right level of safety without restricting adult movement, making it an ideal choice for families.

The innovative auto-close and stay-open features ensure both convenience and security, giving you hands-free access while maintaining a safe barrier. This gate also includes an extra wide walk-thru door for smooth and easy passage, and its sill-free design helps reduce the risk of tripping for young children.

Key Features

  • Adjustable width for stair, doorway, and hallway use
  • Auto-close and stay-open features for convenience
  • Extra wide walk-thru door for easy passage
  • Durable steel construction with double lock design
  • Simple installation with included spacers and accessories

Why Choose Babelio?

Whether you're looking for stair safety gate options or need a doorway baby gate that's both strong and easy to use, the Babelio gate is built to last. Its sturdy steel frame and double lock mechanism provide strong support and prevent accidental openings. The easy installation process means you can set up a safe and functional barrier in no time, giving you peace of mind every day.

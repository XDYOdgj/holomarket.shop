Boppy Side Sleeper Pregnancy Pillow

Designed for the ultimate comfort during pregnancy and beyond, the Boppy Side Sleeper Pregnancy Pillow offers reliable support for side sleeping. Its innovative stretchy center panel gently contours to your growing belly, ensuring you stay comfortable and aligned throughout all trimesters. The soft, breathable fabric made from 80% polyester and 20% cotton provides a cozy and adjustable fit, while the compact design makes it easy to carry for travel or use at home.

  • Supportive design for comfortable side sleeping during pregnancy

Why You'll Love It

This pillow is a trusted companion for mothers, offering support during pregnancy and postpartum. It helps reduce pressure on your back and shoulders, making rest more restful.

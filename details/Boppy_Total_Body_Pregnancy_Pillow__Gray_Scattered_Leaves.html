Boppy Total Body Pregnancy Pillow

Designed for expectant and postpartum mothers, this pillow offers complete support for your body during every stage of your journey. Whether you're growing your family or recovering from childbirth, the Boppy Total Body Pregnancy Pillow adapts to your changing needs with its unique contoured shape.

Ergonomic Support for Your Entire Body

This pillow is engineered to provide full-body support, cushioning your shoulders, hips, and belly while you rest. It offers a more natural and holistic way to stay comfortable and relaxed throughout your pregnancy and beyond.

Perfect for Mobility and Cuddle Time

Unlike traditional U-shaped pregnancy pillows, the Boppy design allows for easy movement and repositioning. It's ideal for sharing moments with your partner or using it independently for rest and recovery without feeling confined.

Soft, Hypoallergenic, and Breathable

Featuring a hypoallergenic fiber fill, this pillow provides a comfortable sleep experience immediately. No chemical odors, no waiting time—just pure relaxation. The soft rayon cover is also breathable and machine-washable, keeping it fresh and clean for long-term use.

Versatile and Multi-Functional

Whether you're dealing with back or hip pain, recovering from surgery, or simply enjoying some downtime, this pillow is a versatile tool. It's perfect for lounging, reading, or bonding with your baby, ensuring comfort that fits your lifestyle.

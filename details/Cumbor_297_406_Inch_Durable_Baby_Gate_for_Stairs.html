Why You Need the Cumbor Baby Gate

The Cumbor baby gate is designed to give you peace of mind with its patented double-lock safety release system. This ensures that your home stays secure, especially when you're juggling responsibilities and your baby is learning to crawl or walk.

It's easy to install without any tools, thanks to the pressure-mounted, self-closing mechanism.

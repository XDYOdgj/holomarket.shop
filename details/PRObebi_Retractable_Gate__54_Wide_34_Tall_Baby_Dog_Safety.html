PRObebi Retractable Baby & Dog Gate

A versatile, safe, and space-saving solution for keeping your baby and pets secure in any home setting.

The PRObebi Retractable Baby & Dog Gate is designed with an adjustable bracket that adapts effortlessly to different baseboards. This ensures a perfect fit without causing any damage to your flooring, making it ideal for modern homes that prioritize clean, unobtrusive design.

Its safe and sturdy double lock mechanism allows for a firm, secure closure with just a simple press and turn. Whether you're keeping your little ones away from the stairs or keeping your pets from exploring restricted areas, this gate offers peace of mind with every use.

The one-handed operation feature makes it incredibly convenient to use while holding your baby or carrying items. This gate can be installed at the bottom of stairways, doorways, or hallways with ease, and its streamlined design ensures quick setup without disrupting your daily routine.

With no bottom rails, the PRObebi gate minimizes tripping hazards, making it a safe option for both indoor and outdoor use. Its retractable mesh design also saves valuable space when not in use, making storage and accessibility effortless.

  • Adjustable bracket for seamless baseboard fit
  • Expandable size from 0-54" wide and 34" tall
  • Double lock mechanism for secure closure
  • One-handed operation for easy use while holding baby or carrying items
  • No bottom rails to prevent tripping hazards

Regalo 2-in-1 Wall-Mounted Baby Gate

Keep your little one safe while maintaining freedom of movement. This durable and adjustable gate is perfect for doors, hallways, and the top or bottom of stairs.

Safe & Versatile Design

Engineered with safety-lock technology, this gate ensures a secure fit and reliable protection for your baby. It's designed to fit openings between 29 to 43 inches wide, making it ideal for various home environments.

Whether you're looking to keep your child away from stairs, protect a doorway, or create a safe boundary in the hallway, this gate is built for performance and peace of mind.

With no assembly required, this gate is ready to install in minutes, and its easy-to-remove design makes it simple to store or reposition as your home needs change.

Durable Materials

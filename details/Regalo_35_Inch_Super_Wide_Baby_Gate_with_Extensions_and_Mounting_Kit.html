Regalo 35-Inch Baby Gate

The Regalo 35-Inch Baby Gate is a top choice for parents and pet owners who want both safety and style. With an extra wide design that fits most door and stair openings between 29 to 35 inches, it ensures comprehensive coverage for your home. Its walk-through door opens both directions, making it easy to move through while keeping your little ones and pets secure. The high-end bronze finish adds a touch of elegance to your home decor without sacrificing strength and durability.

This gate is built for versatility, perfect for use in hallways, doorways, and the bottom of stairs. It's ideal for children aged 6 to 24 months and also provides a safe barrier for medium to large dogs. The included extension kits allow you to customize the fit to your specific space, and the gate folds flat for convenient storage. The safety-lock and secure hardware ensure reliable protection, meeting all safety standards and giving you peace of mind.

Retractable Baby Gate with 3-Way Locking System

Create safe, enclosed spaces in your home with this reliable Retractable Baby Gate by Perma Child Safety. Whether you're keeping your baby safe on the stairs or protecting your pets from restricted areas, this gate is built to adapt to your unique needs.

Standing at 33\" tall and 71\" wide, it fits a variety of doorways and staircases with ease. The 3-Way Locking System offers flexibility: lock the gate securely, unlock it quickly for emergency access, or fully unlock when you need full passage. This ensures convenience without compromising safety.

The gate features a strong, stain-resistant mesh that's both durable and designed for long-term use. It's resistant to UV damage and can withstand harsh outdoor conditions and even pets' claws. This makes it the perfect solution for parents and pet owners alike.

Easy installation is a breeze with our included spacers and hardware mount, allowing you to secure it around baseboards or other obstacles. The one-handed operation makes setup quick and hassle-free. It's not just a gate — it's a smart, essential barrier that helps you maintain control of your living space safely and efficiently.

Key Features

  • Retractable design for easy access and storage
  • 3-Way Locking System: Lock, Quick Unlock, Full Unlock
  • Durable, stain-resistant mesh with UV protection
  • Suitable for infants 6-24 months and pets up to 40 lbs
  • Hardware mount for secure installation indoors or outdoors

Perfect For

Parents and pet owners looking to ensure safety without sacrificing convenience.

Use in entryways, stairs, front porches, and back decks for maximum versatility.

Ideal for preventing access to dangerous areas, keeping pets out of restricted spaces, and creating safe play zones.

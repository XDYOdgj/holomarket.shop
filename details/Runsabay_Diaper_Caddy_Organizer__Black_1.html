Runsabay Diaper Caddy Organizer

This handy organizer is designed to keep your baby's daily essentials neatly sorted and easily accessible, no matter where you are. Whether you're at home, on the go, or preparing for a baby shower, it's the perfect companion for parents who value both convenience and style.

With a spacious interior and 2 zipped pockets, you can store diapers, wipes, baby clothes, and other important items in one organized place. The neutral gray color looks great in any nursery layout, and the waterproof felt material ensures it's safe for your little one and durable for years of use.

Summer Infant Extra Tall Decor Safety Gate

Keep your baby or pet safe while maintaining the aesthetic of your home with this versatile and stylish Summer Infant safety gate. Designed for use in doorways, hallways, and even at the top of stairways, it offers secure protection without compromising on design.

The gate's impressive 36"" height and adjustable width ranging from 28"" to 38.25"" make it suitable for a variety of spaces. Its feature-rich design includes a one-handed walk-thru door for easy access, a built-in door stopper for added safety, and a sleek gray finish that blends seamlessly into modern interiors.

Whether you're navigating daily routines or simply managing household traffic, this gate provides peace of mind with its sturdy build and user-friendly installation. No-drill mounting options and included hardware make setup simple, while third-party safety testing ensures reliability.

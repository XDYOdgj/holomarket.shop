Summer by Ingenuity Doorway 48W Safety Gate

Keep your home safe and secure with the Summer by Ingenuity Doorway 48W Safety Gate. Designed for families and pet owners, this versatile gate offers reliable protection for doorways, stairways, and hallways up to 48 inches wide.

Key Features

  • Easy installation with a no-drill pressure mount system
  • Adjustable width to fit openings between 29' and 48'
  • Auto-close mechanism for safety and convenience
  • Secure lock design with narrowly spaced bars to prevent small hands or paws from getting caught
  • Durable build with safety testing and approval from third-party organizations

Why Choose This Gate?

Whether you're protecting your little ones or keeping your furry friends out of restricted areas, this gate combines functionality with modern design. The door swings both ways and stays open when opened more than 90 degrees, giving you flexibility during busy moments. Plus, the built-in stopper prevents the gate from swinging over the top of stairs, adding an extra layer of security.

With its sleek look and user-friendly features, the Summer by Ingenuity Doorway 48W Safety Gate is the perfect choice for any family looking for a safe and stylish solution to keep their space secure.
